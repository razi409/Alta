Education demand has been increased continuously since the human bin decided to explore the world more and more. the education has changed over the years and these days there is a huge competition between education seekers in order to improve their knowledge and skills to achieve the highest level in their future career. in order to achieve this. students are not only looking for the best school or universities to become educated but also they are looking for extra courses or classes to help them in their educational life to get the highest marks respect to other students or cover their weaknesses in their lectures. 
on the other hand, there are many educated people who are looking for a job or needs extra income for their life. the popular career for educated people is to share their knowledge and become a teacher.
for both side is too hard to find the best teachers or more students easily, fast and 
Alta School Application is a platform to share these demands and needs so those who want to be a private teacher or those who are looking for a teacher can find each other on this platform.

<h3>Communication with Services</h3>

<img src="https://github.com/razi409/Alta/blob/master/images/backend.png?raw=true">

Firebase offers a lot of services, like Authentication, Storage, Real time databse, Analythics. In Alta Schools project, all backend system is 
depened on Firebase. Firebase Real time Databased which is a NoSql database, Storage, Authentication are some of the services that 
Alta Schools project is takin advantage of. Also it is good to mention that the RestFull Communication to the backend is handled through
Firebase API, which creates an easy base for communication and data transition.
Alta Schools has two method of sign ups, one is through Google API, by choosing a Google account, and the other is through Facebook
API by entering Facebook credentials by the End user.
